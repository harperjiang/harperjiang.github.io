Hao Jiang is currently a sixth year Ph.D student. Hao's advisor is Professor Aaron J. Elmore

Hao got an B.S. in Computer Science at Fudan University, an M.S. in Computer Science at Clarkson University.

You can find my CV here.

Research Interest

Hao's research primarily focus on distributed system and database. He is currently working on the following problems:

  • Lightweight Encodings in key-value stores
  • Lightweight Encoding Selection based on column-oriented dataset features
  • Structure Discovery and Extraction from column-oriented datasets
